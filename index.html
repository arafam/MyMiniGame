How to Play:
Level 1: Move the mouse to control the basket and catch falling fruits. You need atleast 20 fruit to advance. Avoid grenades!
Level 2: Your basket becomes a player that shoots arrows at falling fruits. Missing 10 fruits or hitting 10 grenades causes you to lose (and restart at Level 1), while grenades kill you immediately. The bird is a power-up that slows things down (arrow can't hit it, get it yourself while avoiding grenades). Hold down "Space" to rain arrows or click to shoot it.
