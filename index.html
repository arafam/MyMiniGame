How to Play

Click the screen to play the Audios.

Level 1:
Move the mouse to control the basket and catch falling fruits.
You need at least 20 fruits to advance or keep playing to beat the "High Score" count.
If you missed a fruit the count will decreament. Avoid grenades!

Level 2:
Your basket becomes a player that shoots arrows at falling fruits. Missing 10 fruits or hitting 10 grenades causes you to lose (and restart at Level 1), while grenades kill you immediately.
The bird is a power-up that slows things down (arrow can't hit it, get it yourself while avoiding grenades).
Hold down "Space" to rain arrows or click to shoot it.
